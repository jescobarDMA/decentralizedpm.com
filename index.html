The Founder
Jose Escobar
Founder & CEO — Decentralized Property Management

After studying Electrical and Computer Engineering, Jose built systems for industrial automation — PLC control logic, sensor networks, distributed controls. Then he encountered residential property management.

The inefficiency was identical: smart people doing repetitive work without systems to support them. Owners waiting on monthly calls for numbers they should see in real time. Tenants submitting requests with no acknowledgment. Vendors uncoordinated. Reports nonexistent.

DPM exists to fix that — by running property operations the way a good engineering team runs a system: with documentation, accountability, and zero tolerance for undocumented failure.
